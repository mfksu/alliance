About

How it all started





Alliance Accounting’s birth traces back to a singular moment of inspiration, coupled with unwavering determination for success. Owner and Operator, Alex Evans embarked on her accounting journey in 2011, having earned her Master’s in Accounting from Kennesaw State University in Kennesaw, GA. Subsequently, she honed her skills under the tutelage of esteemed CPAs within the SaaS industry. Following her pivotal role in facilitating the sale of those enterprises, Alex transitioned to corporate finance in the medical device sector, contributing to the financial landscape of a prominent medical device manufacturer.

Our story




Alliance Accounting was established to provide accounting and finance services to businesses so they can concentrate on their profitability and leave the administrative burden of number crunching to the professionals. Since it’s inception in 2019, Alliance has helped several small businesses thrive and do what they do best – grow and earn profits.



Our approach




Our vision is to create sustainable, strategic solutions, for business owners so they can concentrate on growing their profits – not pushing paper around. We provide personalized services for each client’s needs. Whether initiating a new venture or navigating the pathways of expansion, our steadfast commitment is to engage in collaboration, working hand in hand to propel each business owner toward the realization of their goals.
